Easy Fried Rice

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Rice1 cup
Eggs2
Vegetable oil2 tsp

Steps:

  1. Cook rice in boiling water until tender, then drain and cool.
  2. Heat oil, cook eggs into an omelet, chop, and set aside.
  3. Mix all ingredients in a wok, stir-fry for 5 minutes, and serve.

Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Milk1 cup
Egg1

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, milk, and egg into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  3. Cook each side until golden brown and serve with syrup.
